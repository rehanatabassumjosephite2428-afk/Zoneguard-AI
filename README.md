# 🚀 ZoneGuard AI

> AI-powered parametric insurance for gig workers — built to protect income, not assets.

---

## 📌 Inspiration

Gig delivery workers operate in highly unpredictable environments. In cities like Chennai, sudden rain, extreme heat, or hazardous air quality can immediately reduce their ability to work, directly impacting their earnings.

Unlike traditional employees, gig workers lack financial protection against such disruptions.

This led us to a simple idea:

> What if income protection could be automatic, based on real-world conditions instead of manual claims?

ZoneGuard AI was built to answer that.

---

## ⚙️ What it does

ZoneGuard AI is an AI-driven parametric insurance platform that:

- 📡 Monitors environmental disruptions (rain, heat, AQI)
- 🧠 Calculates **dynamic weekly premiums**
- ⚡ Automatically triggers claims based on thresholds
- 💸 Simulates **instant payouts** without user intervention

### 🧮 Pricing Model

$$
\text{Weekly Premium} = f(R_l, D_f, T_s)
$$

Where:
- \( R_l \): Location risk level  
- \( D_f \): Disruption frequency  
- \( T_s \): Trust score

## ⚠️ Challenges we ran into

- Defining **meaningful disruption thresholds**  
- Balancing affordability vs sustainability  

$$
\text{Sustainability} \propto \frac{\text{Premium Collected}}{\text{Claims Paid}}
$$

- Lack of real-world datasets  
- Simulating real-time APIs  
- Designing fraud detection without live data  
- Time constraints during development
